Privacy Policy

Square Maze

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Anderson Binário ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses and shares information when you play Square Maze (the "Game"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information. The Game uses third-party services that may collect information as described below.

2.1 Device and Usage Information

Through Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, the following may be collected automatically:

  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings
  • App version and session information
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data
  • In-app events (e.g., level completions, feature usage)

This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the Game.

2.2 Advertising Identifiers

The Game displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS) and related information to show relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

2.3 In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We do not receive or store your payment information.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve the Game
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To display advertisements that help keep the Game free

4. Third-Party Services

The Game integrates the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchases — Payment information is handled entirely by Google or Apple.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Data retained by third-party services is governed by their respective retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad personalization for users identified as children is disabled in compliance with applicable law (COPPA, GDPR-K). If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Game, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since we do not directly store personal data, most requests should be directed to the relevant third-party services listed in Section 4. For questions, contact us below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
